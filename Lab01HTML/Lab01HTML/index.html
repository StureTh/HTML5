Story Time

Since i was a little kid i always loved computers and gaming in general. When i was five years on Christmas eve i gott my first gaming console a Super Nintendo 8 Bits. Later about one year or so my dad came home with the first home computer to our family. It always helped me relax and be creative in my tought processes, Like how do fix this problem or solve certain puzzle. There is nothing more satisfying than to solve a problem you been sitting with for hours sometimes even days before it clicks.

It wasnt untill about six months ago i discoverd the world och programming and software development. I started to read more about it and tought, this maybe is the stuff i can enjoy and work with for years to come. I found the School "EC-Utbildning" and they offered a Web developer program that i started this spring. Even though im still very new on this topic I really enjoy it and having a blast learning more and more everyday. Sometimes it feels like all the years of reading about computers in general and all the puzzles solved help me learn even quicker.
